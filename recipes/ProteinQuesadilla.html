Protein Quesadilla

Finished Protein Quesadilla

This quesadilla is jam-packed with a whopping 48g of protein! Very easy to make, great for a quick lunch or serve with guac for a laid-back dinner.

Prep time: 5min

Cooking time: 10min

Total time: 15min

Ingredients

  • 2x Carb Balance Tortillas
  • 1/2 Cup Mexican-Style Melting Cheese
  • 4oz ground beef
  • Fajita Seasoning
  • Handful of Diced onion (optional)
  • Avocado Ranch (optional)

Instructions

  1. Set your pan to med-high heat and begin cooking the ground beef.

    Make sure to use your spatula to break the beef into small chunks, and season to taste with the fajita seasoning.

  2. While the ground beef is cooking, take out your first tortilla

    (optional: spread a serving of Avocado Ranch on the tortilla)

    Weigh out a 1/4 cup of cheese onto the tortilla.

  3. When the ground beef is cooked through, add to the top of the cheese on the tortilla.

    (optional: This is when you can add the handful of diced onion)

  4. Add the last 1/4 cup of cheese to the top of the ground beef, and close the quesadill with the second tortilla.
  5. Put your quesadilla back on the pan, cook until cheese is melted through (3=5min each side)
  6. Serve and enjoy!

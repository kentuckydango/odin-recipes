Toast

The Perfect Toast

This toast is jam-packed with a whopping 2g of protein! Very easy to make, great for a quick lunch or serve with butter for a laid-back breakfast.

Prep time: 0min

Cooking time: 1min

Total time: 1min

Ingredients

  • 1x Slice of Bread
  • 1x Extra Slice of Bread (optional)
  • Butter (optional)

Instructions

  1. Place the slice of bread into your toaster.

    (optional: most toasters are able to cook two slices of bread simultaneously. If desired, place two slices into your toaster at this time)

  2. Wait for your toast to cook.

    (chef's note: most toasters will notify you when the toast is complete. Go and get some chores done while you're toast is being made!)

  3. Remove toast from the toaster, serve, and enjoy!

    (optional: add butter to your toast for an exciting new dimension of flavor and texture!)
